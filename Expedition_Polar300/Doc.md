Alex's field notes: I encountered 3 penguins on my expedition. They had very different personalities. Jessica was very scrappy, she kept starting arguments with the other penguins and stealing their fish. Scott was a lover at heart with a gentle disposition. Fernando was a homebody; he likes to stay by his favourite rock all day. Sometimes the other penguins come to him, but he seems fine on his own as well. A lovely trio overall.
